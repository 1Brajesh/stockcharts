Market Cap: $39.08B P/S: 0x CONSUMER CYCLICAL AUTO MANUFACTURERS

Honda Motor Co., Ltd. (HMC) is a leading global automotive and motorcycle manufacturer based in Tokyo, Japan, well-known for its commitment to innovation and sustainability. The company is actively transitioning towards electrification, backed by a comprehensive portfolio that includes vehicles, power equipment, and advanced technologies. Honda's strategic global presence across North America, Europe, and Asia, combined with its rigorous quality standards and efficient supply chain, ensures its competitive edge in the dynamic automotive market. With a focus on environmental initiatives and cutting-edge mobility solutions, Honda continues to redefine the future of transportation.

🐐 Assets = Liabilities + Equity

Translation: What Jerry owns = What Jerry owes + What's actually Jerry's

Or as Jerry mutters every April during tax season:
"Everything I have equals everything I owe plus whatever scraps are left for me, a humble goat man just trying to make cheese in this cruel world."
Total AssetsEverything Jerry owns: 12 acres of land, a barn that's "rustic" (rotting), 47 goats, Sergio (appraised at $800, priceless to Jerry), the cheese cave, and a 2004 Ford F-150 held together by duct tape and prayer.

Total Shareholder EquityIf Jerry sold absolutely everything, paid off every debt, and finally returned that tractor... this is what he'd have left. His actual net worth. Some days it's a retirement fund. Other days it's "enough for a bus ticket and a fresh start."
