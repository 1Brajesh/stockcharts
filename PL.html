Planet Labs PBC

Market Cap: $12.00B P/S: 138x Ownership — Insider: 1.9% | Institutional: 73.0% | Retail: 25.1% INDUSTRIALS AEROSPACE & DEFENSE

Planet Labs PBC (Ticker: PL) is a leading provider of satellite imagery and geospatial data, renowned for its innovative constellation of Earth-observing satellites that delivers critical insights across diverse industries, including agriculture, forestry, and urban planning. Founded in 2010, the company is committed to democratizing access to satellite data, enabling organizations to make informed decisions through precise assessments of environmental changes. With continuous monitoring capabilities, Planet Labs supports global sustainability initiatives and is strategically positioned in the rapidly growing remote sensing and analytics market, emphasizing its dedication to leveraging technology for data-driven solutions.
