Stellantis NV

Market Cap: $31.92B P/S: 0x Ownership — Insider: 23.7% | Institutional: 45.9% | Retail: 30.4% CONSUMER CYCLICAL AUTO MANUFACTURERS

Stellantis NV, headquartered in Lijnden, Netherlands, is a prominent global automotive manufacturer formed by the merger of Fiat Chrysler Automobiles and PSA Group. The company features a diversified portfolio of well-known brands, offering a range of passenger cars, trucks, SUVs, and light commercial vehicles, while prioritizing innovation and sustainability. Stellantis is proactively advancing its electric and connected vehicle initiatives, aligning with market trends and consumer demands. As the automotive industry faces transformative changes, Stellantis is dedicated to enhancing mobility through advanced technologies and optimizing operational efficiencies, positioning itself for sustainable long-term growth.
