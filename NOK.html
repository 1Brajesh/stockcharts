Nokia Corp ADR

Market Cap: $36.84B P/S: 8x TECHNOLOGY COMMUNICATION EQUIPMENT

Nokia Corporation (ADR) is a prominent global telecommunications leader headquartered in Espoo, Finland, renowned for its cutting-edge solutions in fixed and mobile networks, particularly in 5G technology, software, and the Internet of Things (IoT). The company plays a crucial role in facilitating digital transformation for communication service providers and enterprises, enhancing both connectivity and operational efficiency through its extensive product portfolio. With a strong commitment to sustainability, Nokia is integral to advancing the telecommunications industry while driving innovation that supports global connectivity and sector-wide performance improvements.

Translation: What Jerry owns = What Jerry owes + What's actually Jerry's
