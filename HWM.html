Howmet Aerospace Inc

Market Cap: $88.41B P/S: 42x INDUSTRIALS AEROSPACE & DEFENSE

Howmet Aerospace Inc. is a prominent player in the aerospace and transportation sectors, headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The company focuses on delivering advanced engineered solutions, specifically high-performance components for jet engines, including titanium structures and critical fasteners, alongside forged aluminum wheels for heavy-duty vehicles. With a strong emphasis on innovation and sustainability, Howmet Aerospace integrates state-of-the-art technology to improve operational efficiency and address the dynamic needs of its diverse global customer base. As a key industry leader, Howmet Aerospace is instrumental in shaping the future of aviation and transportation.

Translation: What Jerry owns = What Jerry owes + What's actually Jerry's

Or as Jerry mutters every April during tax season:
"Everything I have equals everything I owe plus whatever scraps are left for me, a humble goat man just trying to make cheese in this cruel world."
Total AssetsEverything Jerry owns: 12 acres of land, a barn that's "rustic" (rotting), 47 goats, Sergio (appraised at $800, priceless to Jerry), the cheese cave, and a 2004 Ford F-150 held together by duct tape and prayer.

Total Shareholder EquityIf Jerry sold absolutely everything, paid off every debt, and finally returned that tractor... this is what he'd have left. His actual net worth. Some days it's a retirement fund. Other days it's "enough for a bus ticket and a fresh start."
