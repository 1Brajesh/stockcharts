Intuitive Surgical Inc

Market Cap: $173.36B HEALTHCARE MEDICAL INSTRUMENTS & SUPPLIES

Intuitive Surgical, Inc. is a premier American medical technology firm, recognized for pioneering robotic-assisted surgical systems, notably the da Vinci Surgical System. The company is dedicated to enhancing patient outcomes through the innovation of minimally invasive surgical technologies, complemented by a diverse range of instruments and extensive training initiatives for healthcare practitioners. Boasting a strong pipeline of technological advancements, Intuitive Surgical is strategically positioned to leverage growth opportunities in the rapidly evolving robotic surgery sector, promoting improved surgical precision and expedited patient recovery.
