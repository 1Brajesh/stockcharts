Analog Devices Inc

Market Cap: $151.07B BE: 50x TECHNOLOGY SEMICONDUCTORS

Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI) is a prominent multinational semiconductor company based in Wilmington, Massachusetts, known for its expertise in high-performance analog, mixed-signal, and digital signal processing integrated circuits. Catering to a wide array of industries—including automotive, industrial, communications, and consumer electronics—ADI provides cutting-edge solutions in data conversion, signal processing, and power management. The company's relentless focus on innovation and its robust research and development initiatives enhance its reputation as a leader in the semiconductor space, driving advancements in product performance and energy efficiency. With a proven track record of growth and a strategic vision for future advancements, ADI is well-positioned to capitalize on emerging market opportunities.
