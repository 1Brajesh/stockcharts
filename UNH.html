UnitedHealth Group Incorporated

Market Cap: $305.02B HEALTHCARE HEALTHCARE PLANS

UnitedHealth Group Incorporated is a premier American multinational managed healthcare and insurance company based in Minnetonka, Minnesota. As the largest insurance provider by net premiums and the second-largest healthcare entity by revenue, generating $257.1 billion as of 2020, UnitedHealth Group boasts a robust portfolio, with its UnitedHealthcare division contributing approximately 80% of total revenue. The company is strategically positioned to leverage the evolving healthcare landscape, demonstrating a commitment to innovation through investments in digital health, telemedicine, and data analytics, ultimately enhancing patient care while driving growth and operational efficiency.
